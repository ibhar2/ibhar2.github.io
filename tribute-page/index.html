Keanu Reeves

"The One"

Biography

Keanu Charles Reeves is a Canadian actor, director, producer, and musician. Best known for his acting career, he has starred in films such as the Bill and Ted franchise, Speed, Point Break, The Devil's Advocate, and The Matrix series. He is a frequent and generous contributor to all sorts of charitable causes. Having witnessed his younger sister's battle with Leukemia, he has set up a private foundation that helps aid cancer research and a couple of children's hospitals. His first name means "cool breeze over the mountains" in Hawaiian. He also may or may not be immortal so there's that...

The timeline of the man known as Keanu

  • (563 BC-483 BC) Born a prince named Siddharta, he turns his back on his comfortable and protected life, and sets out on a journey to solve the problem of universal suffering. Through many trials, he attains enlightenment and becomes the Buddha.
  • (748-814) Becomes Karolus Magnus (Charlemagne, Charles the Great). Lays foundations for modern France, Germany, Belgium, and the Netherlands. Takes the Frankish throne and becomes king of Italy, the first Holy Roman Emperor. After uniting Western Europe, he mysteriously dies and is hurriedly buried the same day.
  • (1847-1922) Going by the name Paul Mounet he studied to become a medical doctor before pursuing a career in acting. He allegedly dies in 1922 although his body is never found.
  • (1989-1991) Co-founder of the band Wyld Stallions alongside his bud Bill S. Preston Esq, they travel through time in order to pass their history final. After much philosophizing and most excellent adventuring, they go on to become "The Great Ones", inspiring millions with music and bodacious air guitaring.
  • (1992-1994) Has a successful career as a SWAT officer saving a bus full of people and Sandra Bullock from a crazed bomber. Joins the FBI and goes undercover as a surfer to thwart a string of bank robberies.
  • (1999-2003) Working as a computer programmer who moonlights as a hacker, he learns that he lives in a simulated world and joins a group of rebels who fight the machines. Turns out to be "The One", the person prophesied to lead the insurrection of the humans against the machines. Learns kung fu and ultimately sacrifices himself to save humanity ala Jesus.
  • (2014-current) Living the retired life of a hitman, he returns when Russian mobsters kill his dog, a gift from his late wife. A lot of guns and shenanigans ensue.
